What is Governance?!

The traditions and institutions through which power is exercised in states for the public good, including the process of selecting, monitoring and removing those in power, the ability of governments to manage resources and implement sound policies effectively, and respect by both citizens and the state for the institutions that govern economic and social interactions in Between them
