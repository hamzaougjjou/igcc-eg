General Sports Authority: “Bin Salman” supports Saudi clubs with 2.5 billion riyals

Prince Abdulaziz bin Turki Al-Faisal, Chairman of the General Sports Authority, announced the allocation of two and a half billion Saudi riyals to support Saudi clubs to ensure their financial and administrative sustainability.
Al-Faisal revealed, at the Saudi Club Support Strategy Conference, yesterday, that 1.64 billion riyals had been identified to support Saudi Professional League clubs, with 50 million riyals in direct support for each club, 20 million riyals in support linked to developing the governance system, and 10 million support linked to developing infrastructure and facilities. Clubs, 5 million for events accompanying matches, 3 million to increase the club’s fans, and 1 million for each match related to public attendance at hosted matches.
